Masala Dosa in Bengaluru

By admin
on 20.04.2009

It must be something in the genes. Mallus and Tams are like peas in a pod. Especially when it comes to food.

So for a poor mallu lad fed up with the over - oiled Masala dosas of Bangalore where is the refuge? Obviously a Tamil hotel. And so it was that I found Pongal at Jeevan Bhima Nagar. 22 Rs for a decent masala dosa. Not bad :)
